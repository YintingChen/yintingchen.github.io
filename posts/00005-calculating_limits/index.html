二零二三年十月 微积分 侦探小说 新概念

今日晴朗，阳光驱散多日阴云，天蓝气冷。

1 #

早上自然醒后，将昨日余下的《低智商犯罪》之近30章看完。

这本书的主人公张一昂颇有些韦爵爷的风范。可惜紫金陈的其他作品我近乎都在2015年那个在北京的夏天读过，时间久远，情节和人物已无法清晰记得。所以无法回忆起张一昂跟在系列主人公高栋身边破案时的事迹，并拿之与这本以张为主角的书中，其亲身指导和经历破案的风采作比较。

固然我们要承认张一昂运气过人，但是更重要的是，他选择了一条正确的道路。

设想，若其为偷鸡摸狗之徒，行盗做贼之辈，那么这些偶尔的运气也会像这本书里其他角色一样，跟着你走短短一段路，然后突然不见，杀你个措手不及。

而且张一昂虽然破案能力高低犹可商榷，但是其对高栋的忠心，对下属的敲打和照顾，对人心的拿捏，包括其语言的艺术，确实是令人在饱笑之余不禁躬身自省。

一天内擒获三江市首富团伙、文物走私集团、连环爆炸抢劫二人组，这种非比寻常的功绩也许其师傅高栋都未能成就，而不走寻常的张一昂却与此相搭，非常自然。

张德兵回头举枪就开，“砰”一声，张一昂屁股血肉炸开一片鲜红，他不管不顾，一翻身跳到了周荣身上，将其一把拉过，匕首架上脖子，喝道：“住手！动一下就杀人了！”他屁股一阵剧痛，瘸腿将周荣拉到墙壁边，把周荣挡在他面前。

而其本质上的一腔正气，在大是大非上的毫不含糊，更是令人想起韦爵爷。

“放了她。”张一昂目光对向李茜，这时，李茜也割开了绳子站起来，另两名持匕首的小弟拦在了她面前。“我叫你放她出去！我数到三，一——二——”

无论是面对群豪，在身体受绑、生死关头之际，还是屁股中弹后，又在医院遭遇杀手袭击，张一昂始终以保护下属为己任，虽然跟这个下属是公安部副部长的侄女这一点上有所关系。

眼见他跳下来用匕首直刺李茜，张一昂一把扔掉拐杖，扑过去抱住霍正。一手抓住他左手，一手死命握住他持刀的右手，两人都摔倒在地。霍正胡乱用刀向旁边划，张一昂的手臂和胸口出现了多道口子，鲜血将衣服染成了红色。

读这本书时，常常有 “时来天地皆同力” 之感，作者就是那个天地，读者也是那个天地。书中围在张一昂身边的下属们也是那个天地，在省厅里保护张一昂的上司们也是那个天地。

什么样的人，大家都喜欢，什么样的人，大家都愿意保护他，希望他成功，为他的成功感到开心？

在我看来，这个人一定会是一个不装的真诚的人。

晚上背诵了新概念第三册的第58课《A spot of bother》。 下面我试着全文默写出来。

Lesson 58 A spot of bother
Question: What did the old lady find when she got home?

The old lady was glad to be back at the block of flats/apartments where she lived. Her shopping had tired her and her basket had grown heavier with every step of way home. In the lift/elevator, her thoughts were on lunch and a good rest, but when she got out at her own floor, both were forgotten in her sudden discovery that her front door was opened. She was thinking that she must reprimand her home help the next morning for such a monstrous piece of negligence when she remembered that she had gone shopping after the home help had left and she knew that she had turned both keys in their locks. She walked slow into the hall and at once noticed that all the room doors were open, yet following her regular practice, she had shut them before going out. Looking into the drawing/living/sitting room, it was as clear as daylight then that the burglars had forced an entry during her absence. Her first impulse was to go around all the rooms looking for the thieves, but then she decided that at her age, it might be more prudent to have someone with her, so she went to fetch the porter/doorman from his basement. By this time her legs were beginning to tremble, so she sat down and accepted a cup of very strong tea. Then, her composure regained, she was ready to set off/set out with the porter’s assistance to search for any intruders who might be still lurking in her flat.

They went through all the rooms, being careful to touch nothing, as they did not want to hinder the police in their search for fingerprints. The chaos was inconceivable. She had lived in the flat for thirty years and was a veritable magpie at boarding, and it seemed as though everything she possessed had been tossed out and turned over and over. At least sorting out things she should have discarded years ago was now being made easier for her. Then a police inspector arrived with a constable and she told them of her discovery of the ransacked flat. The inspector began to looking for fingerprints while the constable checked that the front door locks had not been forced, thereby proving that the burglars had either used skeleton keys or entered over the balcony. There was no trace of fingerprints but the inspector found a dirty red bundle that contained jewelry which the old lady said was not hers. So their entry of the flat was apparently not the burglars’ first job that day and they must have been disturbed. The inspector then asked the old lady to try to check what was missing by the next day and advised her not to stay alone in the flat for a few nights. The old lady thought he was a fussy creature, but since the porter agreed with him, she rang up her daughter and asked for her help in what she described as a little spot of bother.

下面以3个例子来复习图最右边的2个方法。

例子1：\(\lim\limits_{x \to -1} \frac{x+1}{\sqrt{x+5}-2}\) #

\(\frac{x+1}{\sqrt{x+5}-2} = \frac{x+1}{\sqrt{x+5}-2} * \frac{\sqrt{x+5}+2}{\sqrt{x+5}+2} = \frac{(x+1)(\sqrt{x+5}+2)}{(\sqrt{x+5})^2-2^2} = \frac{(x+1)(\sqrt{x+5}+2)}{x+1} \)

例子2：\(\lim\limits_{\theta \to 0} \frac{1-cos\theta}{2sin^2\theta}\) #

\(\frac{1-cos\theta}{2sin^2\theta} = \frac{1-cos\theta}{2(1-cos^2\theta)} = \frac{1-cos\theta}{2(1+cos\theta)(1-cos\theta)}\)

例子3：\(\lim\limits_{\theta \to -\frac{\pi}{4}} \frac{1+\sqrt{2}sin\theta}{cos2\theta}\) #

\(\frac{1+\sqrt{2}sin\theta}{cos2\theta} = \frac{1+\sqrt{2}sin\theta}{cos^2\theta-sin^2\theta} = \frac{1+\sqrt{2}sin\theta}{1-2sin^2\theta} = \frac{1+\sqrt{2}sin\theta}{(1+\sqrt{2}sin\theta) (1-\sqrt{2}sin\theta)}\)
