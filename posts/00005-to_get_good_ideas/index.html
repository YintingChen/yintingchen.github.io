To get good ideas

Dec 09 2023
2023-12 creating

I prefer to talk with different people to get various kinds of ideas. Many times, I gain what I have not expected but interesting enough to let me start some new work.

Also, I like to go to some places I have never been or revisit some from where quite a long time since I came back. These new environments can invariably trigger my senses to catch something new and unique. I normally bring a small notebook together with me, in order to write down everything which comes to my mind.

I like writing by hand. Feeling the patterns generated from my pen on the paper alway gives me some kinds of comfort. But, of course, in this digital time and era, mobile phones are very convenient when we need to record some memories with photos or videos.

I also listen to a lot of music of different kinds of genres everyday, like what I am just doing now, listening to music and writing my post. When I encounter some very interesting patterns, which do not need to be in the same song, I will note down the names of songs and the time when the patterns show up, for I will try to analyze them later on this day.

Books, movies, and games also provide me a great number of chances to get inspired. I can go out from my own life and put myself into the character (no need to be the protagonist) to feel the logic and stories inside the material. This gives me an opportunity to lead a different life. This is why I like reading books, watching movies and playing games, which contain a solid story. Especially, the joy times when I discuss the contents with my friends. What kind of decisions I would make when I am faced with those situations, which character I like the most, which I hate the most and so on. These are good topics to generate a conversation which can inspire me to write down some good ideas.

The last one is keep creating whatever you want to create. After these absorbing, man should use what he has learned to create something which totally belongs to him. Otherwise, in my opinions, these absorbing would became pointless, for man does not build his own work up on them but just waste all of his time on the work by other people.

For me, the process of creation also gives me a valuable chance to talk to myself, to analyze the materials I learned before based on my views of value. This always calms me down and I can incredibly feel there is an another myself, watching me when I use my mind to try to create something intriguing, which totally belongs to me. I like this cooling feeling, for it makes me feel I am living.
